About me

Passionate programmer since the age of 7, big data lover, pragmatic developer addicted to high efficiency in the development process. My ambition is to improve people's lives using modern information technologies such as Social Networking, Mobile Applications and the Semantic Web. Today, I'm developing whyd, a new web-based communication tool that leverages linked data.

I've also been a drummer for about as long. Some of my bands are listed below.
