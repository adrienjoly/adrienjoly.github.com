What I’m doing now

(This is a now page, and if you have your own site, you should make one, too.)

I’m home in Montreuil, Paris area, France. It's getting cold and dark outside!

After working 4.5 years as the lead software developer of a startup company (Whyd), and freelancing for 1.5 years, I'm now developing my own projects.

What keeps me busy these days:

  • Preparing the teaching materials for my JavaScript programming course at EEMI, starting in November.
  • Finalizing my MOOC on how to create a startup the Lean way, in French. (based on a presentation we gave at NUMA, Paris)
  • Developing productivity products: Next Step for Trello, 1st Thing In The Morning, Snoozer, Freelance Directory, and one following the Running Lean methodology (user-centered).
  • Maintaining Openwhyd (fork it on Github), and coordinating its contributors.
  • Practicing with my new Jazz band!
  • Feeling happy about my freedom, and increasingly enjoying solitude.
  • And still sharing ideas on Pitchcard

My mission is to create useful tools for people and myself. My objective is to make a living out of it! ^^

If I find myself unable to bootstrap alone, I'll look for a skilled extrovert that I can trust, to team up with!

When my activities or priorities change, I’ll update this page.
